Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use EZMenu to switch materials if my product has other feature menus?

Yes, EZMenu can coexist with other features. You can customize how the material switch menu is triggered, such as through link messages or listen messages.

How do I change a material’s texture without modifying the entire material settings?

Use the Set PBR Texture node. Choose the texture type to replace (e.g., Base Color, Normal, (O)RM, Emissive), and provide the new texture UUID. This will only change the specified texture without affecting other material parameters.

How do I change a material’s texture and adjust the texture’s scaling, offset, and rotation?

Use the Set PBR Texture Adv node. Choose the texture type to replace (e.g., Base Color, Normal, (O)RM, Emissive), and provide the new texture UUID. Additionally, you can adjust the texture’s scaling, offset, and rotation parameters.

Can I place permission-controlled menu items in a submenu instead of the main menu?

Yes. You can connect submenu items to the permission-controlled menu.

Can the menu item that opens the permission-controlled menu have a custom name?

Yes. Enter the desired menu item name in the MenuItem property.

How do I delete unnecessary menu items?

Simply delete the text of the unnecessary menu item. The corresponding link will also be removed.
